About me

I started learning the guitar by myself at the age of twelve, playing mainly blues and rock.

My classical training started at the academy of Ottignies (Belgium) with percussion, piano and finally classical guitar in the class of Annie Cattelain.

Between 2000 and 2001, I studied Jazz at the Jazz Studio of Antwerp and from 2001 classical guitar at the IMEP in the class of Maria-Nieves Mohino. I graduated in June 2006. Between 2007 and 2010, wishing to broaden my horizons, I followed courses of jazz ensemble and fretless bass in the class of Nathalie Lorier at the Academy of Eghez√©e.

Between 2010 and 2020, I spent a lot of time perfecting my recording and production techniques in my home studio. I also played guitar in different formations, notably for the female vocal ensemble "Kava-Kava" and "Duo Nebraska", a duet of piano and electric guitar. I also recorded two self-produced albums.

Currently, I teach guitar at the Conservatory of Namur and spend a lot of time composing and feeding my Youtube channel which is entirely dedicated to the sharing of scores, arrangements and compositions for guitar.
